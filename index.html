Overview

RaceFi is the first AI/ML car racing game on Solana ecosystem. Within RaceFi's metaverse, players can earn while enjoy various game mode or make profit by owning in-game property. Pure race or Battle race, PvP or PvE, racers or gamblers, the possibilities are endless.

Gameasset

  • Racetrack Land & Track List

    Owners of race track land earn race fees each time a race happens.
    Each race track is divided into 100 land plots.
    The earning from a race will be divided evenly.

  • Cars

    Cars are the core assets of the game,
    as they are required to play the game.
    Players buy cars from the verified RACEFI Car Collection on the Polrare Marketplace

  • Land

    A land is a place that brings many resources
    you won't want to miss this opportunity.

  • Gasstation

    This will be a useful place when your car runs out of energy.

  • Garage

    The second rarest asset in the game.

  • Billboard

    This is the place to help you know what you need to do on that land.
